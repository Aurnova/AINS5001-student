# AINS5001: Your workspace

**You are in the right place if** you are taking **A Modern Approach to AI** and your instructor asked you to use **GitHub Classroom** and **Codespaces** for exercises.

**Instructor materials** (full book narrative, lecture notes, solutions, IMS cartridges, grading keys) are **not** published on this site. Your instructor shares those through **Castalia**, the LMS, or other faculty channels.

## Do this first

1. **Accept the GitHub Classroom assignment** your instructor sent (you will get a **private** repo for each exercise or unit).
2. **Open that repo in GitHub Codespaces** (or clone locally with the same `.devcontainer` if you prefer).
3. **Work in `exercises/`** — follow the README in your assignment repo; tests and autograding hooks are part of the template.

## Lectures (public)

- **[Jupyter Book slide index](https://inquiryinstitute.github.io/aima/slides/)** — slide-shaped pages with narration audio when available (same content order as the course; **not** a replacement for your instructor’s syllabus or grading).

## Workspace template (long-lived)

Many cohorts use a personal fork of **[aima-codespace](https://github.com/InquiryInstitute/aima-codespace)** renamed to `aima-<your-github-username>` for ongoing practice alongside weekly Classroom assignments. Your instructor will say if that applies to you.

```{note}
Need the faculty view (IMS CC, solutions, instructor guide)? That lives on the **[instructor hub](https://aurnova.github.io/AINS5001/)** — not required for completing exercises.
```
